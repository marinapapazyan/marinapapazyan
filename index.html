MARINA PAPAZYAN CURRENT INDEX PAST
Marina Papazyan is a writer and artist from Istanbul. They mostly work with video and sound, and write fiction and essays. They have been interested in how monstrousness and disease are represented and intersect, with vampirism and lycanthropy appearing as recurring motifs in their writing. Werewolves, vampires and satyrs take on different roles in exploring queer genders and erotics, set against the contradictory legacies of (post-)Ottoman imperialisms, and the liberation struggles that resist them. Until recently, they were a program coordinator at the non-profit art space Depo (2021–⁠2025), and helped run BAS Artists’ Book Collection (2020–⁠2025). They are currently an MFA candidate in Writing at Bard College.
