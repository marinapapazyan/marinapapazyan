MARINA PAPAZYAN INDEX CURRENT PAST
Marina Papazyan is an artist/-writer living in between Istanbul/-The Hague. She works on issues of vulnerability and grief through the collecting, arranging and voicing of found images and documents. Lately she has focused on processes of survival and extinction, drawing particularly from her own body, family and kin. Her most recent work could be characterized as fiction, where she writes specifically on the complications of being together and permeable to one another.
